20th Century Boys

This time Urasawa chose to make a much less grounded story. 20th Century Boys is a longer story that spans generations focusing on an everyman named Kenji whose normal life gets taken away as he is forced to deal with a Japanese cult that plans on taking over the world. This is very much a character driven story and it wouldn't work if Urasawa wasn't capable of creating a large cast of compelling and interesting characters. If you want to check out a story full of political intrigue, conspiracies, and extensive character growth then this is for you.
